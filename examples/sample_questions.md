# Sample Questions

Copy any of these into the chat to try the assistant. They work with the included demo documents in `samples/` — copy those files into `data/approved_documents/` and rebuild the knowledge base first.

---

## Quality Management (ISO 9001)

**General understanding**
- What is the scope of the quality management system?
- What is the quality policy?
- Who is responsible for maintaining the quality manual?

**Processes and procedures**
- How are customer complaints handled?
- What is the process for approving new suppliers?
- How are nonconforming products controlled?
- What records must be kept after product release?

**Corrective actions (CAPA)**
- What is the difference between a corrective action and a preventive action?
- How do I raise a CAPA?
- What root cause analysis methods are approved?
- How long do I have to close out a critical CAPA?
- What happens if a corrective action is found to be ineffective?
- How is CAPA effectiveness measured?

**Audits and reviews**
- How often are internal audits conducted?
- What inputs are required for management review?
- What metrics are tracked for quality performance?

**Objectives and KPIs**
- What are the current quality objectives?
- What is the target on-time delivery rate?
- What is the acceptable internal rejection rate?

---

## Tips for Better Answers

- Be specific: *"What is the CAPA close-out time for a Critical finding?"* gets a better answer than *"CAPA times?"*
- Ask about document sections: *"What does the CAPA procedure say about root cause analysis?"*
- Ask follow-up questions: the assistant maintains context within a session
- If the answer cites a source, ask *"Can you expand on that section?"*

---

> **Note:** The assistant only knows what is in the uploaded documents. If a question falls outside your document set, it will say so rather than guessing.
