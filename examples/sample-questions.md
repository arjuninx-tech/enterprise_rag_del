# Sample questions

Upload the two documents in this directory, rebuild the knowledge base, and try
the following questions.

## Quality management

- What is the scope of the quality management system?
- What is the quality policy?
- Who maintains the quality manual?
- How are customer complaints handled?
- How are new suppliers approved?
- How are nonconforming products controlled?
- What records must be kept after product release?

## Corrective and preventive action

- How do I raise a CAPA?
- Which root-cause analysis methods are approved?
- How long can a critical CAPA remain open?
- What happens when a corrective action is ineffective?
- How is CAPA effectiveness measured?

## Audits and reviews

- How often are internal audits conducted?
- Which inputs are required for management review?
- Which metrics track quality performance?

Specific questions usually produce better retrieval results. The assistant
should report that reliable evidence was not found when the indexed documents
do not contain an answer.
